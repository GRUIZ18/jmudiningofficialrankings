JMU DINING - THE OFFICIAL RANKINGS

Derivative of extensive research and studies

By Dukes - For Dukes

First Place

E - Hall

Sitting as truly the undisputed G.O.A.T. of JMU Dining, E - Hall has it all for simply a punch. From a classy aura to a ridiculous variety of ice cream flavors, E - Hall popped off. Located behind the Skyline dorms, it's subtle appearance is all to humble for the greatness inside


Notable Dishes

  • Grilled chicken
  • Custom bowls
  • Chicken nugget Thursday
  • Various ice cream flavors
  • Cheese steaks (about every 2 weeks)

Drawbacks

  • On east campus
  • Limited vegan options
  • No tator tots

Second Place

Dukes Dining

Tucked between Carrier Library and The Union, Dukes Dining houses several big name restaurants such as Panda Express, Panera, Denny's, Oath Pizza, Blueridge Bowl, and Tacodillo. With so much to choose from, it's hard to miss with both punch and dining dollar options.


Notable Dishes

  • Chicken and rice
  • Mac and cheese
  • Burgers and fries
  • Pizza
  • Custom bowls

Drawbacks

  • Can be very busy
  • Limited portion sizes
  • Expensive when using dining dollars
  • Only Denny's is open for dinner

Third Place

Festival

Resting above the east campus commons, Festival features a food court with pasta, fast food, Chinese, and acai bowls. Every individual item can be bought for a punch.


Notable Dishes

  • Pasta bowls
  • Burgers and fries
  • Acai bowls

Drawbacks

  • On east campus
  • Limited vegan options
  • Limited serving sizes
  • Lack of food quality from certain stations
  • Can have long lines
  • Easily repetitive

Fourth Place

D - Hall

Placed in the center of campus for all to enjoy, D - Hall features a massive buffee with Chic - Fil A, Freshens, Java, and Market 64 beneath. The buffee boasts weekly rotations of different foods and the options below allow for both dining dollar and punch deals. Students can use excess punch at market 64 to buy small food items.


Notable Dishes

  • Custom bowls
  • Fried chicken and tator tots
  • Mac and cheese and grilled cheese
  • Chic - Fil - A
  • Freshen's smoothies

Drawbacks

  • Extremely busy during meal times
  • Taste fatigue
  • Lack of quality in some foods
  • Chic - Fil - A line is crazy long
  • Java is a ghost town. Wonder why...

Fifth Place

Bistro 1908

Found on the second floor of the Student Success Center, Bistro 1908 has an impressive rep despite its small size. Comprised of an Italian and fast food station, both menus offer punch or dining dollar options.


Notable Dishes

  • Pasta bowls
  • Burger and fries
  • Pizza
  • Stromboli

Drawbacks

  • On the edge of campus
  • Limited options
  • Long waits during lunch hours
  • Closed after 3pm
